The Problem

THE DATA GAP IN ATHLETICS

Built by high schoolers from Oak Park, CA

Good coaching needs real numbers. Timing gates give you split data — how fast an athlete accelerates, where their top end is, how they compare week to week. Without that, you're guessing. Hand timing is off by a few tenths at best. That's not nothing. In sprinting, that's everything.

The schools that can afford the tools get the data. Everyone else makes do. We got tired of that being the way it works.

Commercial Systems

$350 – $1,500+ per unit, often with monthly subscription fees. Accessible only to elite and well-funded programs.

EqualSplit

$79.99. No subscription. Two timing modes so you can measure what actually matters — acceleration splits and full speed profiles. Made for every athlete.

A portable, self-contained sprint timing system built on LiDAR sensing. Measures acceleration splits, top-end speed, and full sprint profiles with millisecond-level precision. USB-C rechargeable, 100m+ wireless range between two gates, custom 3D-printed enclosure. No app. No subscription. Just data.

Two Timing Modes, One Device
Mode 1 — A to B Split

Start at gate one, finish at gate two. Get a clean split time — perfect for measuring reaction off the line or timed fly-ins.

Mode 2 — Speed Profile

Multi-split breakdown of your full sprint — see where you're accelerating, where you're hitting top end, and where you're losing it.

On a tight budget or represent an underfunded program? We want to help. Contact us directly — no one gets left out because of cost.

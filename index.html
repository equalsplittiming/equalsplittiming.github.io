The Problem

THE DATA GAP IN ATHLETICS

Built by high schoolers from Oak Park, CA

Good coaching needs real numbers. Timing gates give you split data — how fast an athlete accelerates, where their top end is, how they compare week to week. Without that, you're guessing. Hand timing is off by a few tenths at best. That's not nothing. In sprinting, that's everything.

The schools that can afford the tools get the data. Everyone else makes do. We got tired of that being the way it works.

Times are dropping at the high school, collegiate, and professional levels — and advancing technology, including timing gates, is a big part of why. We're working to equal the field, so that edge isn't reserved for the programs that can pay for it and talent in underserved communities can reach its full potential.

A portable, self-contained sprint timing system built on LiDAR sensing. Measures acceleration splits, top-end speed, and full sprint profiles with millisecond-level precision. USB-C rechargeable, 100m+ wireless range between two gates, custom 3D-printed enclosure. No subscription. Just data — with a companion mobile app on the way for saving and tracking results over time.

Two Timing Modes, One Device
Mode 1 — Gate 1 to Gate 2 Split

The clock starts when Gate 1 is passed and stops when Gate 2 is passed. A clean split time — perfect for flys (10m, 20m, 30m), 40-yard dashes, and more.

Mode 2 — Speed Profile

A multi-split breakdown of your full sprint. The timer starts on the release of Button 2 and records button release to Gate 1, Gate 1 to Gate 2, and button release to Gate 2 — a breakdown of acceleration and top-end in one run.

Our Journey

FROM ZERO TO PRODUCT

Early 2026
The Idea

It started with a frustration. We were student athletes who knew what good timing data could do — and watched our school make do without it. So we started sketching. What would it actually take to build our own?

First Build
Proof of Concept

We taught ourselves everything — microcontrollers, LiDAR sensors, wireless communication. The first prototype was rough, but it worked. A real split time on a real track. That was enough to know we were onto something.

Now
LiDAR Timing Gate

Custom PCB, refined firmware, a purpose-built 3D-printed enclosure. Two timing modes. 100m+ wireless range. Everything a coach needs, at a price any program can afford.

The Problem

THE DATA GAP IN ATHLETICS

Precision timing gates give coaches objective split data — acceleration, top-end speed, reaction time. Without them, athletes rely on hand timing that can be off by 0.2+ seconds. That gap is the difference between actionable coaching and guesswork.

Privileged programs get the data. Everyone else guesses. We're changing that.

Our Journey

FROM ZERO TO PROTOTYPE

Early 2026 — The Beginning
Zero Hardware Experience

Started with nothing but an idea and YouTube tutorials. Learned microcontrollers, LiDAR sensors, and 3D design from scratch using Tinkercad. Identified that most athletes can't access the timing data elite programs take for granted.

V1 Prototype
Proof of Concept

First build used ultrasonic sensors connected to a laptop — clunky, but it produced real split times. Proved the concept worked. Our engineering teacher and track coach were both impressed. Tested with teammates in real conditions.

Current Build
LiDAR Timing Gate v2

TF-Luna LiDAR at 250Hz, ESP32 microcontroller on perf board, 1.3" LCD display, USB rechargeable, 100m+ wireless range, custom 3D-printed enclosure. Fully self-contained. Two timing modes. Shared the project with Olympic gold medalist Hunter Woodhall — his response: "Mad scientists. Inspirational."

V2 — Coming Soon
Full PCB + Scale

Moving to a custom PCB for a more durable, lower-cost, fully manufacturable build. Refined enclosure. Targeting $55 in parts. Mission: with every few units sold, donate one free to a school, club, or community that can't afford it.
